My Experience




1. My Experience at Konica


At Konica Photo Studio in Buchach, Ukraine (2018–2022), I worked as a photo editor, refining both technical and creative skills. My responsibilities included editing photos using Photoshop, ensuring a seamless and professional finish, and selecting the best-quality images for client projects. I managed multiple tasks under tight deadlines, collaborated with my team, and consistently delivered work aligned with clients’ expectations. This experience not only honed my technical abilities but also taught me how to effectively manage time and communicate professionally.



2. Volunteering at the Ukrainian Cultural Centre ‘Dnipro’


Between March and October 2022, I volunteered at the Ukrainian Cultural Centre in Manchester, supporting newly arrived Ukrainians. I provided guidance on resolving their cases, assisted in distributing essential supplies like medicine and protective equipment, and communicated in Ukrainian, Russian, English, and Polish to address diverse needs. Handling customer concerns and offering step-by-step solutions improved my communication and problem-solving skills, while contributing to such meaningful work deepened my understanding of community service.



3. Placement at Community Revival


During my college placement at Community Revival, I worked as an IT assistant, gaining practical experience in technology and design. My tasks included creating visually appealing poster designs, managing social media marketing campaigns to boost engagement, and troubleshooting and repairing computer devices. This placement gave me a chance to apply my technical skills to real-world problems while also developing my creativity and teamwork abilities. It was a valuable learning experience that prepared me for future challenges in IT and design.
