About Me

I am a Brighton-based student currently enrolled in the T Level Transition program - EDEXCEL Level 2. I specialize in both photography, particularly portrait photography, and information technology. I enjoy spending much of my time on the road, capturing the world through my lens and working on various IT projects.


.

I am also comfortable with other industry-standard tools, which helps me bring creative projects to life with precision and flair.


My combined skills in IT and photography enable me to approach projects with a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic vision, making me a versatile and valuable asset in any creative or technical endeavor.
